KnightStream

A 2D adventure/platformer following a young knight in search of mystical swords protected by elemental guardians. Defeat the guardians and confront the Dark Knight to save the kingdom.

Project Description
  • Developed by a team of 8 students.
  • The goal: Create a 2D platformer with unique boss battles. Completion of each level rewards the player with new abilities for the main character.
  • Inspirations: Super Mario, Dead Cells, Cuphead
Project Responsibilities
  • Created major development milestones for each department (programming, art, design, and sound).
  • Developed backlog and task list for development team to begin production.
  • Prioritized backlog of each department based on most valuable features.
  • Oversaw burndown charts and team member velocity to create data-driven model to project management.
  • Conducted the daily stand-up, sprint review, sprint post-mortem, and all other team meetings.
